Hi 👋 My name is Nikola Vracevic
================================

Data Science, Software Development
----------------------------------

At the begining of my bachelor in Urban/Regional planning, I developed a strong interest in cartography and Geographic Information Systems (GIS). Eventually, I noticed that in top-tier scientific papers, authors were going beyond GIS and they were applying R/Python geospatial libraries or machine learning algorithms. That, along with the countless repetitive tasks in QGIS/ArcGIS that were driving me crazy, prompted me to choose and learn R it is known for its capabilities in scientific computing. I even authored a paper on urban sprawl indices calculated using R. With a goal to be recognized scientist in the community by following current trends in reasearch, I started a graduate double degree Erasmus Mundus programme in GeoData Science and Geoinformatics. Beside knowledge aquired during my studies in remote sensing, geoApp development, databases, big data, machine learning, computer vision among many others, I have been simultaneously working for almost two years in the research lab where I refined my software development and machine learning skills.

*   🌍  I'm based in Salzburg, Austria
*   🖥️  See my portfolio at [vraikonen.github.io](http://vraikonen.github.io/.)
*   🚀  I'm currently working on [my master thesis.](http://link-to-thesis-github)
